03 — The handoffs
Where the baton passes.

When these break, everyone blames the wrong thing.

Growth → Content
Whatever Growth is selling in the ads — the content inside the app must deliver on that promise. If the ad and the content don't match, trust breaks before the habit forms.
Growth → Product
When a new audience is cracked, that information must reach Product. The app has to be ready for whoever the ads bring in — a great CPI means nothing if the product isn't built for those users.
Product → Content
Product knows where users are going in the app. That signal must reach Content. Content serves where users already are — not where no one visits.
Product → Business
Retention is what builds LTV. Business can't grow revenue without Product holding P30. Business sets the pricing — Product makes the experience worth paying for.
